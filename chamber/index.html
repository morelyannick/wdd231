Who We Are

Founded to support the economic vitality of Abidjan, the Abidjan Chamber of Commerce brings together entrepreneurs, small businesses, and large corporations around a shared goal: fostering growth, innovation, and collaboration within our business community.

Since our founding, we have supported our members through networking events, training programs, partnership opportunities, and increased visibility with the public and investors.

  • Foster exchanges among local entrepreneurs
  • Promote the economic development of the region
  • Provide resources and support to members
  • Create lasting partnership opportunities
